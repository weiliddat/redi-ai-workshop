# Workshop Survey

Share the survey link in chat right after the wrap-up. Give students time to fill it in before the call ends — don't leave it as homework.

**Tool:** Google Forms (or similar). Keep it short — 5 questions, under 3 minutes to complete.

---

## Questions

**1. What's one thing you learned today that changed how you think about using AI?**

(Free text)

_Why we ask:_ Shows whether the core message landed — that how you use AI matters more than whether you use it.

**2. Which learning approach are you most likely to try in your own work?**

- Asking conceptual questions ("why does this work?")
- Breaking tasks into steps and solving each one
- Explaining code back to yourself before using it
- Using AI for hints instead of full solutions
- Something else (please describe)

_Why we ask:_ Tells us which strategies resonated and which need better framing next time.

**3. How confident do you feel about using AI as a learning tool (not just a shortcut)?**

- Very confident — I have a clear approach
- Somewhat confident — I know what to try but need practice
- Not very confident — I'm still unsure how to do it well
- Not confident at all

_Why we ask:_ Measures self-assessed readiness. Low confidence signals we need more practice time or clearer examples.

**4. What was the most useful part of the workshop?**

- Intro: Workshop goals, and what AI can do
- Hands-on: build a chat client (1)
- Theory: Research findings
- Theory: Learning approaches and demos
- Hands-on: extend your chat client (2)
- Conclusion: Recap, do's and don'ts

_Why we ask:_ Helps us allocate time better in future sessions.

**5. Would you recommend this workshop to a friend or classmate?**

- Definitely yes
- Probably yes
- Probably no
- Definitely no

_Why we ask:_ Simple signal of overall value.

---

## Time

- 4 min — students fill in survey before leaving the call

If you remove the `onmessage` handler, **all incoming WebSocket messages will be completely ignored**. This breaks:

1. **Chat message display** – Messages from other users (`type: 'message'`) won't appear in the chat window.
2. **System notifications** – Join/leave announcements (`type: 'join'`/`'leave'`) won't be shown.
3. **Error handling** – Server-sent errors (`type: 'error'`) won't be displayed.
4. **JSON parsing errors** – Invalid messages won't trigger the fallback `'Invalid message received'` message.

The form still **sends** messages (via `socket.send`), but you'll see no responses or updates from the server, making the chat appear broken or one-way. The WebSocket connection remains open, but all incoming data is discarded unprocessed.
